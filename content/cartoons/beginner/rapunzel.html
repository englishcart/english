Questions ‚ñº

1. Where did Rapunzel live?

Answer: b) In a tower

Answer: a) Because her mother stole parsley

Answer: b) Using her hair

Answer: b) To be a hero

Answer: a) She made medicine for his eyes

Answer: b) In Justin's village

10. Did they live happily ever after?

Answer: a) Yes

Answer: a) lives
