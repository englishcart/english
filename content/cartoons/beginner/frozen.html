Grammar ‚ñº

Present Simple:

What Anna do after she meets Hans?

Answer: does

Plurals:

There many people in Arendelle today.

Answer: are

Interrogative Pronoun:

is the Queen of Arendelle?

Answer: Who

Possessive Adjectives:

This is sister's coronation.

Answer: her

Past Simple:

Hans the Princess of Arendelle with his horse.

Answer: hit

Verb "Have Got":

I a lot to do today.

Answer: have got

To Be:

Anna at the gates before they open.

Answer: is

There Is / There Are:

a lot of people waiting for the coronation.

Answer: There are

This/That/These/Those:

is my favorite place in Arendelle.

Answer: This

Present Continuous:

Hans to apologize to Anna right now.

Answer: is trying

Sentence Building ‚ñº

1. gates / will / the / open / soon

Answer: The gates will open soon.

2. I'm / actually / okay

Answer: I'm okay, actually.

3. I / to / ready / am / go

Answer: I am ready to go.

4. Princess / Arendelle / of / Anna / is / the

Answer: Anna is the Princess of Arendelle.

5. apologize / for / I / hitting / you

Answer: I apologize for hitting you.

6. are / ready / they / for / coronation / the

Answer: They are ready for the coronation.

7. I've / hours / been / up / for

Answer: I've been up for hours.

8. you / are / sure / Anna ?

Answer: Are you sure, Anna?

9. I / with / apologize / my / horse / for / hitting / you

Answer: I apologize for hitting you with my horse.
