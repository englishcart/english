Pluto's Puppy-Sitting Adventure (Welcome to the clubhouse)

Duration: 1.34-3.37 minutes

Directions ‚ñº

1.

READ the VOCABULARY

It is important to read the vocabulary before you watch the video. This will improve your ability to understand the video. It will also help you understand how the new vocabulary is used naturally.

2.

WATCH the VIDEO

The first time you watch the video, just try to understand the overall situation.

3.

ANSWER the QUESTIONS

First, try to answer all the questions from memory. Then rewatch the video and try to answer the questions that you missed.

4.

WATCH and READ the SCRIPT

Watch the video again while you read the script. Reading and listening at the same time will help you hear each individual word and improve your listening accuracy.

5.

DO the ACTIVITIES

There are several different activities that focus on listening accuracy, pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structure.

Vocabulary ‚ñº

Questions ‚ñº

1. What is Pluto?

Answer: a) A dog

2. What is the puppy's name?

Answer: b) Bella

3. What is Clarabelle holding?

Answer: c) A puppy

4. What does "puppy-sitting" mean?

Answer: b) Watching the puppy

5. Who would love to help Clarabelle?

Answer: a) Mickey

6. What should Mickey do to the puppy?

Answer: c) Give her a bath

7. Who asks about the puppy's name?

Answer: c) Mickey

8. When will Clarabelle return?

Answer: c) Soon

9. What should Mickey do next?

Answer: b) Feed her lunch

10. What does Mickey say to Clarabelle?

Answer: b) Goodbye

Grammar ‚ñº

1. What article should be used before "clubhouse"?

Mickey: Welcome to clubhouse, everybody.

Answer: c) The

2. Which demonstrative pronoun is used here to point to the dog?

Mickey: is my favorite dog, Pluto.

Answer: a) This

3. Which word should be used to refer to "name"?

Clarabelle: Her name is Bella. name is very cute!

Answer: a) This

4. What is the correct form of the verb "to be" in this sentence?

Clarabelle: I'll be back as soon as I able to.

Answer: a) Am

5. What is the correct form of "to be" for this question?

Mickey: What Clarabelle holding in her hands?

Answer: b) Is

6. Which word should be used to refer to the puppy in this sentence?

Clarabelle: We need to play fetch with puppy.

Answer: a) This

Sentence Building ‚ñº

1. my / is / dog / Pluto

Answer: Pluto is my dog

2. name / her / Bella / is

Answer: Her name is Bella

3. play / fetch / with / I / her / will

Answer: I will play fetch with her

4. back / Clarabelle / will / soon / be

Answer: Clarabelle will be back soon

5. puppy-sit / love / we / to / would

Answer: We would love to puppy-sit

6. bath / nice / give / her / a

Answer: Give her a nice bath

Discussion ‚ñº

1. Do you like dogs?

2. What is your favorite animal?

3. What would you do if you had to puppy-sit?

4. If you had a puppy, what would you name it?

5. How would you feel if your friend asked you to play fetch with their dog?
