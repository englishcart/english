Directions ‚ñº

1.

READ the VOCABULARY

It is important to read the vocabulary before you watch the video. This will improve your ability to understand the video. It will also help you understand how the new vocabulary is used naturally.

2.

WATCH the VIDEO

The first time you watch the video, just try to understand the overall situation.

3.

ANSWER the QUESTIONS

First, try to answer all the questions from memory. Then rewatch the video and try to answer the questions that you missed.

4.

WATCH and READ the SCRIPT

Watch the video again while you read the script. Reading and listening at the same time will help you hear each individual word and improve your listening accuracy.

5.

DO the ACTIVITIES

There are several different activities that focus on listening accuracy, pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structure.

Vocabulary ‚ñº

    • Questions ‚ñº

    1. Who says "Oh, look at that. Just my size"?

    Answer: a) Mickey

    2. What is Goofy doing when he says, "Whoa, you tricky, little tractor!"?

    Answer: b) Riding a tractor

    3. Who says, "We need the professor!"?

    Answer: c) Donald

    4. What does the professor say to Goofy?

    Answer: a) "Slow down already!"

    5. Who is the first to say "Howdy, girls!"?

    Answer: b) Goofy

    6. What are Daisy and Minnie doing?

    Answer: b) Picking apples

    7. Where does Mickey want to take everyone?

    Answer: a) To the Clubhouse Farm

    8. What does Goofy say after causing a problem with the tractor?

    Answer: a) "I goofed up, Mickey."

    9. Who says, "Don't mention it, pal"?

    Answer: b) Donald

    10. What are Mickey and his friends growing at the Clubhouse Farm?

    Answer: b) Fruits and veggies

    Grammar ‚ñº

    1. Fill in the blank with the correct form of "to be":

    Mickey: Now that we ready, let's head on over to...

    Answer: c) are

    2. Fill in the blank with the correct form of "to be":

    Goofy: I know, Professor, but I don't think it knows!

    Answer: b) is

    3. Fill in the blank with the correct form of "to be":

    Donald: We in a hurry!

    Answer: c) are

    4. Fill in the blank with the correct form of "to be":

    Mickey: We going to stop that tractor!

    Answer: c) are

    5. Fill in the blank with the correct form of "to be":

    Professor: It of no use, boys. Goofy can't hear a word I'm saying.

    Answer: b) is

    Sentence Building ‚ñº

    1. ready / we / now / are

    Answer: We are ready now.

    2. show / I / you / want / to / around / the / farm

    Answer: I want to show you around the farm.

    3. stop / the / tractor / need / to / we

    Answer: We need to stop the tractor.

    4. tractor / too / fast / is / going / the

    Answer: The tractor is going too fast.

    5. show / to / me / how / Donald / stop / the / tractor / to\wants

    Answer: Donald wants to show me how to stop the tractor.

    6. I / goofed / Mickey / up

    Answer: I goofed up, Mickey.

    7. going / farm / to / the / we / Clubhouse\are

    Answer: We are going to the Clubhouse Farm.

    8. stop / the / I / don't / think / it / knows / how\to

    Answer: I don't think it knows how to stop.

    9. too / fast / going / the / tractor / is

    Answer: The tractor is going too fast.
