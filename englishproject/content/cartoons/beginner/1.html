Directions ‚ñº

1.

READ the VOCABULARY

It is important to read the vocabulary before you watch the video. This will improve your ability to understand the video. It will also help you understand how the new vocabulary is used naturally.

2.

WATCH the VIDEO

The first time you watch the video, just try to understand the overall situation.

3.

ANSWER the QUESTIONS

First, try to answer all the questions from memory. Then rewatch the video and try to answer the questions that you missed.

4.

WATCH and READ the SCRIPT

Watch the video again while you read the script. Reading and listening at the same time will help you hear each individual word and improve your listening accuracy.

5.

DO the ACTIVITIES

There are several different activities that focus on listening accuracy, pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structure.

Questions ‚ñº

1. What is Daddy Pig bringing for the picnic?

Answer: c) A picnic basket

2. What does Peppa say about Daddy Pig's tummy?

Answer: a) It's big.

3. What do Peppa and George love to do with the bread?

Answer: a) Feed the ducks

4. What does Mummy Pig say about the bread?

Answer: b) We don't have any more bread.

5. What does Daddy Pig want to do after eating?

Answer: c) Run around

6. What is Mummy Pig afraid of?

Answer: b) A wasp

7. What does Daddy Pig do when the wasp flies around?

Answer: b) He runs away.

8. What does Peppa hope about the wasp?

Answer: a) She hopes it goes away.

9. What did Peppa and Daddy Pig forget to do with the cake?

Answer: b) Save it for the ducks.

10. What does Peppa say to Daddy Pig at the end of the picnic?

Answer: c) You are very lucky!

Grammar ‚ñº

1. Choose the correct form of the verb "to be":

Peppa and her family going for a picnic.

Answer: b) are

2. Fill in the blank with the correct article (a, an, the):

Daddy Pig is bringing picnic basket.

Answer: c) the

3. Choose the correct demonstrative pronoun:

Look, there is little duck pond.

Answer: b) that

4. Fill in the blank with the correct article (a, an, the):

We should run around and get exercise.

Answer: a) a

5. Fill in the blank with the correct form of "to be":

Peppa looking after her little brother George.

Answer: a) is

6. Choose the correct form of the noun (singular/plural):

Peppa and George feeding the ducks.

Answer: b) are

7. Choose the correct demonstrative pronoun:

Peppa loves this cake, but is for the ducks.

Answer: b) that

8. Choose the correct form of the verb "to be":

Mummy Pig very happy about the wasp.

Answer: a) is

9. Fill in the blank with the correct article (a, an, the):

I think I lost strawberry cake.

Answer: c) the

10. Choose the correct form of the noun (singular/plural):

There a lot of wasps near Mummy Pig.

Answer: b) are
